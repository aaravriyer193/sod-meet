Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SODMeet.

Do I need to create an account to join a meeting?
Yes. To maintain strict security protocols and prevent unauthorized access, all users must verify their identity using Google Sign-In before entering any meeting room.
Is SODMeet completely free?
Absolutely. We provide premium features like unlimited meeting durations, screen sharing, and AR filters completely free of charge.
Why can't I access certain rooms like /chairs or /secretariat?
Specific rooms are hard-locked by environment variables to specific organizational email addresses. If your Google account email is not on the authorized clearance list, you will be met with an Access Denied screen.
Do the AR Filters require a fast computer?
We use a hybrid approach. Our 3D DeepAR filters utilize WebAssembly and WebGL to run efficiently. If you are on an older device, you can use our "Native Shaders" which manipulate CSS and SVG matrices, requiring almost zero CPU overhead.
